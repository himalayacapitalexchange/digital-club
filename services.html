Why Tokenised Securities will be a new asset class?

Security tokens are regulated in Europe under MiFID II. The European Securities and Markets Authority (ESMA) has applied ‘transferable securities’, to security tokens. 2018 already witnessed several compliant security token issuances. Countries such as Liechtenstein, Germany, Switzerland, Estonia, Malta, and now the UK are leading as favourable jurisdictions for Security Token Issuances.
